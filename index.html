Why We Matter

🏛️

Regulatory Compliance

KYC ensures Block meets BSA/AML and FinCEN CDD Rule obligations, preventing regulatory action and maintaining banking partnerships.

🔍

Risk-Based Monitoring

Periodic and event-driven reviews identify changes in customer risk profiles, ensuring high-risk customers receive appropriate oversight.

📊

Gatekeeper Function

KYC serves as the first and ongoing line of defense — from onboarding verification through continuous monitoring and screening.

Our Team

KYC analysts require expertise in identity verification methodologies, beneficial ownership structures, PEP/adverse media screening, and risk-rating frameworks across multiple jurisdictions (US, UK, EU, AU). Reviews encompass CDD, EDD, and ongoing monitoring — requiring proficiency in document authentication, corporate registry research, and understanding of complex ownership hierarchies. Unlike transactional operations teams, our analysts assess holistic customer risk profiles and make onboarding/offboarding determinations that balance regulatory compliance with customer experience.
