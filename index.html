The Know Your Customer (KYC) program ensures Block identifies, verifies, and monitors customers throughout their lifecycle. We conduct Customer Due Diligence (CDD), Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), and ongoing screening to prevent financial crime, comply with BSA/AML regulations, and protect Block's ecosystem from illicit actors.

Why We Matter

🏛️

Regulatory Compliance

KYC ensures Block meets BSA/AML and FinCEN CDD Rule obligations, preventing regulatory action and maintaining banking partnerships.

🔍

Risk-Based Monitoring

Periodic and event-driven reviews identify changes in customer risk profiles, ensuring high-risk customers receive appropriate oversight.

📊

Gatekeeper Function

KYC serves as the first and ongoing line of defense — from onboarding verification through continuous monitoring and screening.

Team Composition

Our program operates with a hybrid workforce model combining internal full-time employees (FTEs) with specialized BPO vendor partners (AMLRS, PwC, Guidehouse) across all KYC queues to ensure scalability and operational resilience.
